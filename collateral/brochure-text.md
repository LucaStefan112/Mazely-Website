## [Page 1: The Challenge]

### Visitors Shouldn't Need a Guide to Navigate Your Building

Complex facilities — hospitals with dozens of departments, university campuses spanning multiple buildings, government offices across several floors — create daily wayfinding challenges that impact everyone who walks through your doors.

**The reality today:**
- 1 in 3 first-time hospital visitors struggle to find their destination
- University staff spend significant hours each semester giving directions
- Outdated signage can't keep pace with renovations and departmental moves
- Visitors with mobility challenges or language barriers face even greater obstacles
- Every confused visitor is a missed appointment, a negative experience, or an accessibility gap

Traditional solutions — printed maps, static signage, information desks — are expensive to maintain, impossible to update in real time, and leave visitors without help outside business hours.

## [Page 2: The Solution]

### Mazely: Indoor Navigation That Works

Mazely is a web-based indoor navigation platform that guides visitors through your facility using step-by-step visual directions with real photographs.

**No app download. No special hardware. No confusion.**

Visitors simply scan a QR code at any entrance or checkpoint, select their destination, and follow photo-guided directions on their smartphone.

**How visitors navigate:**
1. **Scan** a QR code at any building entrance
2. **Select** their destination from a searchable directory
3. **Follow** step-by-step visual directions using real corridor photos

It works on any smartphone. No registration. No Bluetooth. No GPS dependency.

## [Page 3: Key Capabilities]

### Built for Complex Facilities

**Visual Step-by-Step Navigation**
Real photographs guide visitors through every corridor, turn, and decision point. No abstract maps to interpret — visitors see exactly what's ahead.

**Multi-Floor Routing**
Seamless navigation across floors via elevators, stairs, and escalators. The system automatically calculates the optimal route including floor transitions.

**QR Code Entry Points**
Place QR codes at entrances, lobbies, elevator banks, and key intersections. Each code serves as an instant starting point for navigation.

**Smart Pathfinding**
Advanced algorithms calculate the shortest, most accessible route between any two points in your facility — in real time.

**Multi-Language Support**
Available in English, Romanian, French, and German. Additional languages available on request for institutions serving diverse populations.

**Usage Analytics Dashboard**
Track navigation sessions, identify popular routes, monitor search patterns, and measure visitor satisfaction — all from a centralized dashboard.

## [Page 6: Deployment Process]

### From Discovery to Live Navigation in Weeks

Deploying Mazely is straightforward and non-disruptive. There's no hardware to install, no network infrastructure to modify, and no app for visitors to download.

**Step 1: Discovery & Planning** (Week 1)
We assess your facility's layout, visitor flow patterns, and wayfinding pain points to design an optimal deployment plan.

**Step 2: Facility Mapping** (Weeks 1–2)
Using our interactive floor plan designer, your team (with our guidance) maps rooms, corridors, elevators, stairs, and key waypoints for each floor.

**Step 3: Visual Content** (Week 2–3)
Photographs are captured for each corridor direction and key decision points, providing visitors with clear visual guides.

**Step 4: QR Code Deployment** (Week 3)
QR codes are generated and placed at strategic locations — entrances, lobbies, elevator banks, major intersections, and department entrances.

**Step 5: Staff Training** (Week 3)
Brief training sessions ensure front desk staff, security, and administrators can manage and update the system confidently.

**Step 6: Go Live & Monitor** (Week 4)
The system goes live. Analytics tracking begins immediately, providing insight into usage patterns and visitor behavior.
