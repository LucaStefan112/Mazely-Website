### Slide 5: How It Works — For Administrators
**Simple Setup. Powerful Results.**

1. **Map Your Facility** — Use our interactive floor plan designer to map rooms, corridors, and key waypoints on each floor
2. **Connect Spaces** — Link rooms and corridors with pathways; connect floors via elevators and stairs
3. **Add Visual Guides** — Upload or capture directional photos for each corridor segment
4. **Deploy QR Codes** — Print and place QR codes at entrances, lobbies, elevators, and key decision points
5. **Go Live** — Visitors scan a code and receive instant, photo-guided directions to any destination

- No hardware installation required
- No app download for visitors
- Full administrative control via web dashboard

---

### Slide 6: How It Works — For Visitors
**Three Steps to Any Destination**

1. **Scan** — Visitor scans a QR code at any building entrance or checkpoint
2. **Select** — Choose destination from a searchable list (room, department, office, etc.)
3. **Follow** — Step-by-step visual directions with real photos guide them to their destination

- Works on any smartphone with a camera
- No app installation, no registration, no Bluetooth
- Supports visitors with limited language proficiency through visual navigation
